Gambá de orelha branca ( Timbu )

Is a small mammal with black fur covering the majority of the body. The face is covered with black and white.

  • Scientific Name: Didelphis albiventris
  • Average Lenght: 278mm(tail 315mm)
  • Average Lifespan: 20 months
  • Habitat: tropical, desert, forest

These animals have a long, tapering tail that has a leathery appearance. Sexual size dimorphism is present in D. albiventris, with males being larger than females. D. albiventris is found primarily in open and deciduous forests and mountainous areas, preferring drier habitats.
